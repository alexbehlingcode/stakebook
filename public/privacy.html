Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Stakebook LLC ("Stakebook," "we," "our," or "us") operates the Stakebook application and website (stakebook.live). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (used for account authentication and communication)
  • Password (stored securely using industry-standard hashing)

When you use the app, we store:

  • Betting data you enter (dates, sports, odds, stakes, outcomes, sportsbooks, journal notes, confidence ratings, closing odds)
  • Settings you configure (bankroll limits, spending limits, alert preferences)

We do not collect or store payment information, government-issued identification, social security numbers, or precise geolocation data.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Stakebook service
  • Authenticate your account and keep your data secure
  • Calculate analytics, performance metrics, and health scores based on the data you enter
  • Send important service-related communications (such as password resets)

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which provides encrypted data storage and secure authentication. We use Row Level Security to ensure that each user can only access their own data. All data is transmitted over HTTPS.

Data Sharing

We do not share your individual betting data with anyone. In the future, we may offer anonymized, aggregated statistics (such as average win rates across all users). If we do, no individual user will be identifiable from this data, and we will update this policy accordingly.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data associated with your account
  • Edit or delete individual bets and settings at any time
  • Delete your account entirely by contacting us at support@stakebook.live
  • Request a copy of your data by contacting us at support@stakebook.live

Cookies and Tracking

Stakebook uses essential cookies for authentication (keeping you logged in). We do not use third-party tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics trackers that follow you across other websites.

Age Requirement

Stakebook is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older (or 21+ where required by local gambling laws). We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If we learn that we have collected data from someone under 18, we will delete their account and associated data.

Responsible Gambling

Stakebook is a bet tracking and analytics tool. We are not a sportsbook and do not facilitate gambling. We include responsible gambling features (spending limits, health scores, and loss streak alerts) to help users maintain healthy betting habits. If you or someone you know has a gambling problem, please call the National Council on Problem Gambling helpline at 1-800-522-4700.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app or via email. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
